# **Pokeverse 🚀**  
### _A Pokémon-Themed Gaming & Utility Platform_  

Pokeverse is an **AI-powered, microservices-based platform** offering a **trivia game, Pokédex, team builder, drawing challenges, and logic puzzles**—all designed for Pokémon fans, casual players, and competitive trainers.  

---

## **✨ Features**  

### 🎮 **Pokémon Trivia Game**  
- Challenge yourself with **lore, movesets, abilities, anime references, and competitive battle questions**.  
- **Multiple difficulty levels**—from beginner to expert.  
- **AI-curated & user-generated quizzes**.  
- **Leaderboards, rewards, and daily challenges**.  

### 📖 **Pokédex**  
- **Detailed Pokémon stats, abilities, evolutions, and movesets**.  
- **AI-powered search** to filter Pokémon by type, ability, or battle role.  

### ⚔️ **Pokémon Team Builder**  
- Build **competitive teams**, analyze **type weaknesses**, and optimize strategies.  
- **AI battle simulations** to test your team against opponents.  

### ✏️ **Pokémon Scribble** (_Multiplayer Drawing Game_)  
- Draw a Pokémon while others guess—like **Gartic Phone but Pokémon-themed**!  
- **Play with friends or join public lobbies**.  

### 🔢 **PokeMatrix** (_Logic Puzzle Game_)  
- **Pattern recognition and strategy-based puzzles** with Pokémon mechanics.  
- **Dynamic difficulty scaling** for an increasing challenge.

## **🛠 Tech Stack**  

| Stack | Technology |
|--------|------------|
| **Backend** | Spring Boot (Microservices) |
| **Frontend** | Next.js |
| **Database** | PostgreSQL + Firebase |
| **Authentication** | Google OAuth2 |
| **Service Discovery** | Eureka |
